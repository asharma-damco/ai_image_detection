# Business Requirements — ai_image_detection
**Updated:** 2026-04-28

## Business Objectives
- BO-01: Provide an internal tool capable of reliably identifying AI-generated or AI-edited images to support content authenticity workflows.
- BO-02: Reduce manual effort required to audit image authenticity across internal systems.

## Business Requirements
- BR-01: The framework must classify images as AI-generated, AI-edited, or authentic.
- BR-02: The framework must return a confidence score alongside each classification.
- BR-03: The solution must be operable by internal team members without specialist ML knowledge.
- BR-04: The framework must be extensible to support additional detection models as the field evolves.
